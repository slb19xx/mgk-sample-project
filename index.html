Mainstream Sellout

Machine Gun Kelly collaborated with legendary producer Travis Barker once again after their first project Tickets to My Downfall debuted at # 1 on the Billboard charts in 2021. Released in March 2022, his 6th studio album Mainstream Sellout also debuted in first position giving him 2 back-to-back # 1 records. The deluxe version was released in June 2022 and features new single More Than Life featuring glaive. Machine Gun Kelly is currently touring the US and Europe in support of the project.

Stream 'Mainstream Sellout'

Good Mourning

Machine Gun Kelly recently starred in Good Mourning, a stoner romcom he wrote, produced, and directed with his frequent collaborator Mod Sun. Realeased in May 2022, the movie follows the misadventures of MGK's character London and his friends as they juggle London's acting career with a hilarious search for his girlfriend Apple throughout LA. The laugh out loud movie can be streamed everywhere.

UN/DN LAQR

Known for his unique nail art as much as his eclectic fashion sense, Machine Gun Kelly launched his own unisex nail polish collection in Decemeber 2021 as another way to express his creative vision. UN/DN's product line continues to grow every few weeks and now features over 20 shades. Recently they debuted their first collab with streetwear brand Lonely Ghost that includes 4 new shades, nail art stickers, and merch.

27 Coffee Club

After playing manny/barista Wes in 2016's Showtime series Roadies, it's no surprise MGK opened up his own coffee lounge in his hometown of Cleveland in August 2020. The decor pays tribute to the group of artists lost too soon at the age of 27 like Jimi Hendrix, Amy Winehouse, Mac Miller, and Kurt Cobain, while the lounge's menu features carefully curated coffee, food, and signature cocktails.

Visit 27 Club Coffee

Life in Pink

In June 2022 Hulu released the Sam Cahill-directed documentary Life in Pink which chronicles Machine Gun Kelly's life over the past 2 years. The film is an in-depth look at the ups and downs of the rising star. Fans old and new get an unprecedented look into the life of the man behind the music reminiscent of MGK's vlogs-before-vlogs-were-a-thing YouTube series Kellyvision.
