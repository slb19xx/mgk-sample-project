Mainstream Sellout

In March 2022, Machine Gun Kelly released his 6th studio album Mainstream Sellout. MGK collaborated with legendary producer Travis Barker once again after their first project Tickets to My Downfall debuted at # 1 on the Billboard charts in 2021. Mainstream Sellout became the duo's 2nd # 1 album, and the deluxe version was released in June 2022. Machine Gun Kelly is currently touring US and Europe in support of his latest project.

Stream 'Mainstream Sellout'

Good Mourning

In May 2022 Machine Gun Kelly starred in Good Mourning, a stoner romcom he wrote, produced, and directed with singer/songwriter Mod Sun. The movie follows the midadventures of MGK's character London and his friends as they search for his girlfriend Apple throughout LA. The laugh out loud movie can be streamed digitally.

UN/DN LAQR

Known for his unique nail art as much as his eclectic fashion sense, Machine Gun Kelly launched his own unisex nail polish collection in Decemeber 2021 as another way to express his creative vision. UN/DN's product line continues to grow every few weeks and now has over 20 shades available. Recently they debuted their first collab with streetwear brand Lonely Ghost featuring 4 new shades, nail art stickers, and merch.

27 Coffee Club

After playing manny/barista Wes in 2016's Showtime series Roadies, it's no surprise MGK opened up his own coffee lounge in his hometown of Cleveland in August 2020. The decor pays tribute to the anomolous group of artists lost too soon at the age of 27 like Jimi Hendrix, Amy Winehouse, and Kurt Cobain, while the lounge's menu features carefully curated coffee, food, and signature cocktails.

Visit 27 Club Coffee

Life in Pink

In June 2022 Hulu released the documentary Life in Pink which chronicles Machine Gun Kelly's life over the past 2 years. The film is an in-depth look at the ups and downs of the rising star and gives fans old and new insight into the life of the man behind the music.
